INDONESIA

Indonesia is home to some of the most stunning mountains in the world,
boasting both active volcanoes and majestic peaks.

Mount Rinjani on Lombok Island is the second-highest volcano in Indonesia,
offering a challenging trek with scenic crater lake views.

Bali's Mount Agung is another prominent peak,
known for its cultural significance and spectacular vistas.
